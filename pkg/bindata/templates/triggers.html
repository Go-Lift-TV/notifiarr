Triggers

Triggers are usually for troubleshooting. Use these to make the client do things that are normally on a cron timer.

Sync Custom FormatsSyncs TRaSH custom formats and release profiles to Radarr and Sonarr.
Send System SnapshotSends a system snapshot.
Stuck Queue Items CheckChecks all your apps for stuck items and sends them if they exist.
Send Dashboard StatesCollects dashboard data from all your apps and sends it.
Send Plex SessionsCollects active plex sessions and sends them if any exist.
Check Lidarr for CorruptionChecks all Lidarr instances' database backups for corruption and sends an update.
