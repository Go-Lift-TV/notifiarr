Media Apps

  • The Plex integration is comprehensive, and many of the settings are configured on the Notifiarr.com website.
  • The Tautulli integration is used to provide a Plex username to custom name mapping in notifications.
  • To disable Plex or Tautulli, simply remove the URL for the application. The client only supports one of each.
