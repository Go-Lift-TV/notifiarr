You may add MySQL credentials to your Notifiarr client configuration to snapshot MySQL service health. This feature snapshots SHOW PROCESSLIST and SHOW STATUS data.
Access to a database is not required. Example Grant: GRANT PROCESS ON *.* to 'notifiarr'@'localhost'
